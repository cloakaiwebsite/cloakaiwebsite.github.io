Which AI Assistant is Better for Interviews? ChatGPT vs Claude vs CloakAI

✍ CloakAI Team 📅 2026-06-15 ⏱ 7 min read

The question "which AI assistant is better" is one of the most searched questions right now — and for good reason. In 2026, there are more AI assistants than ever, each claiming to be the best. But the honest answer depends heavily on what you are using it for. When the use case is job interviews, the comparison looks very different from everyday productivity.

⚡ Short Answer

For general tasks, ChatGPT-4o and Claude 3.5 Sonnet are both excellent. For interview-specific use — especially during a live interview — CloakAI is better than all general AI assistants because it is purpose-built for that moment.

The Big General AI Assistants — How They Compare

Let us start with the AI assistants everyone knows and compare them honestly before introducing specialist tools.

ChatGPT (OpenAI)

ChatGPT is the most widely used AI assistant in the world. For interview prep, it is genuinely excellent. You can give it a job description and ask it to generate the 20 most likely questions, then work through each one. GPT-4o has strong reasoning, handles both technical and behavioural questions well, and can review your answers and suggest improvements. The limitation: it is a chatbot. You have to type into it. It cannot listen to your interview. It cannot run invisibly in the background.

Claude (Anthropic)

Claude is a strong competitor to ChatGPT, especially for nuanced writing and longer documents. For interview preparation, it handles resume feedback and cover letters particularly well. Claude's answers tend to be more thoughtful and less formulaic than ChatGPT's, which is useful when you want answers that sound like a real person rather than a template. Same limitation: it is a tool for before the interview, not during.

Google Gemini

Gemini integrates with Google Workspace and is Google's answer to ChatGPT. For interview prep, it is decent. It is particularly useful if you are preparing for roles that require knowledge of Google products or services. As an interview assistant, it suffers from the same fundamental limitation as all general AI — it has no live interview functionality.

Why General AI Assistants Fall Short During Live Interviews

Here is the core problem with using any general AI assistant during a live interview:

  • Visibility: Any browser tab or app window is visible on screenshare. Opening ChatGPT during an interview immediately shows the interviewer what you are doing.
  • Context switching: You have to stop listening to the interviewer, type the question, wait for a response, and then look back at the interviewer. This breaks the natural flow of conversation.
  • Manual transcription: You cannot just say "answer this question" — you have to type what was asked, losing accuracy in the process.
  • No personalisation by default: Unless you paste your resume every single session, the answers are generic.

Specialist Interview AI Assistants — A Better Category

This is where interview-specific AI tools come in. These are not general AI assistants — they are purpose-built to solve the specific problem of real-time interview support.

The Smartest Approach: Combine Both

The most effective interview strategy uses both types of AI assistant at different stages. Two weeks out: use ChatGPT or Claude to research the company, anticipate questions, and draft your best answers. Practice those answers out loud daily. On interview day: run CloakAI as your invisible safety net. If you blank on a question, if the interviewer goes somewhere unexpected, if a technical problem stumps you — CloakAI has your back without anyone knowing.

The better AI assistant is the one you can actually use when it matters most. General AI is great for preparation. CloakAI is great for performance.

Parakeet also has no free plan — you cannot test it before paying. Its privacy claims ("undetectable") are prominent in marketing but rely on the same mechanisms most AI tools claim, without the OS-level content protection CloakAI uses on Windows. Answers are not as deeply personalised to your specific resume as CloakAI's.

Parakeet AI vs CloakAI verdict: Parakeet is a credible tool with strong multilingual support, but for Indian job seekers it is significantly overpriced. CloakAI offers the same core real-time functionality at a fraction of the cost, with a genuinely free 20-minute trial and deeper resume personalisation.

Chiku AI

Chiku AI is the most direct Indian market competitor to CloakAI. It supports 52+ languages, handles coding rounds, and uses a credit-based model starting at ₹1,199 (+GST).

Where it falls short: The entry price of ₹1,199 + GST (approximately ₹1,415 all-in) still works out far costlier per hour than CloakAI's ₹999 for 3 hours (₹333 per AI hour). Browser-based tools are significantly more detectable during screen sharing because browser windows appear in screen capture by default and tab-switching is visible. CloakAI uses native Windows content protection at the OS level, which browser-based tools fundamentally cannot replicate. Chiku also has a smaller user base (~10K candidates) and limited third-party review presence.

Chiku AI vs CloakAI verdict: Chiku AI gets the India-first positioning right, but the browser-based architecture is a real limitation on screenshare protection. CloakAI is cheaper, genuinely invisible at the OS level, and has a proper 20-minute free trial. For Indian candidates, CloakAI is the better value by both price and reliability.

LockedIn AI

LockedIn AI is a broad career platform with 14+ tools — resume builder, job auto-apply, headshot generator, and an interview copilot. It has a free tier and premium plans starting at $49.99/month (approximately ₹4,200/month). The platform claims an ultra-fast 116ms response time and offers a unique "LockedIn Duo" feature that lets a trusted contact assist you during your interview in real time.

Where it falls short: At $49.99/month, it is one of the most expensive options on the market for regular use. More importantly, user reviews note that LockedIn AI is browser-based and has reported visibility issues — multiple independent reviewers have flagged that interviewers can see the interface when tabs are switched or the screen is shared. The breadth of features (14+ tools) is impressive on paper but means the live interview copilot itself is not the primary focus. LockedIn AI vs CloakAI verdict: If you want a broad career platform with many tools, LockedIn AI covers a lot of ground. If you want the most reliable invisible live interview AI at the lowest cost, CloakAI wins clearly — no subscription, no visibility risk, no monthly commitment, and a free trial.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI assistant is better — ChatGPT or Claude?+
For general tasks and interview preparation, both are excellent. ChatGPT-4o tends to be better at structured answers and coding, while Claude is often better for nuanced writing and longer analysis. For live interview use, neither works invisibly — that is where CloakAI is specifically better.
Can I use an AI assistant during a live interview?+
General AI assistants like ChatGPT and Claude are visible on screenshare and cannot be used discreetly during a live interview. CloakAI is purpose-built for live interviews — it runs as an invisible overlay that your interviewer cannot see, even if you are sharing your screen.
Is CloakAI better than ChatGPT for interviews?+
They serve different purposes. ChatGPT is better for interview preparation — research, question anticipation, and answer drafting. CloakAI is better for the live interview itself — it works in real time, stays invisible, and requires no typing during the conversation.
What is the best free AI assistant for interview prep?+
Google Interview Warmup is a solid free option for basic preparation. ChatGPT's free tier is useful for question research. CloakAI offers a free 20-minute trial for live interview practice — enough to test every feature before a real interview.
Does CloakAI replace general AI assistants?+
No — CloakAI is best used alongside general AI tools. Use ChatGPT or Claude for preparation weeks before. Use CloakAI on interview day as your live support. They solve different parts of the same problem.
