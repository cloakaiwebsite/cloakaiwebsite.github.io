Frequently Asked Questions

Will the interviewer see CloakAI when I share my screen? +
No. CloakAI is completely invisible to anyone viewing your shared screen. It uses a system-level method that prevents the overlay from being captured by any screenshare software. The interviewer on Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet will see absolutely nothing.
What if I share my Entire Screen instead of a window? +
CloakAI remains invisible even when you share your full desktop. The invisibility works at the screen output level, not the application level. It does not matter whether you share a window, monitor, or entire screen.
How does CloakAI hear the interviewer's questions? +
It listens through your microphone or computer audio. AUTO mode detects when someone speaks and processes automatically. Manual mode lets you hold Alt while the question is asked and release to send.
When do I receive my license key? +
Your license key is generated automatically the moment payment is confirmed and sent to your email. Fully automated, within seconds, any time of day.
Can I use the same key on two computers? +
No. Each license is bound to the first computer it activates on. If your hardware changes, contact support@cloakai.in.
Does it work on Mac? +
Yes. CloakAI works on Windows 10/11 and macOS. Download the Mac version from our website.
How is CloakAI different from Parakeet AI? +
CloakAI uses a hardware-level display exclusion that Parakeet AI does not - invisible even to the Windows Snipping Tool and OBS. CloakAI is also significantly cheaper (from ₹200/hr vs ₹400+/hr) and offers exclusive features: full session transcripts, 5-answer history browse mid-interview, screen capture analysis, and deep context-aware tailoring.
What is your refund policy? +
All sales are final because license keys are delivered instantly. We strongly recommend testing with the free trial before purchasing. See our full Refund Policy for limited exceptions.
How do I contact support? +
Email support@cloakai.in. We respond within 24 hours. Include URGENT in the subject for interview-day issues.
