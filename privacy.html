Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025  ·  Governs all data associated with CloakAI services.

CloakAI is built on a privacy-first principle. We collect the absolute minimum data necessary to operate the service. Your audio, conversations, and AI responses are never stored by us.

1. Who We Are

CloakAI is an assistive productivity application distributed via cloakai.in. For privacy enquiries, email: support.cloakai@gmail.com. We respond 24/7 over email.

2. Data We Collect

Data TypeWhy We Collect ItWhere It's Stored
Email addressTo deliver your license key and provide supportGoogle Sheets (access-controlled)
Hardware fingerprint (hash)To bind your license to your device (prevents sharing)Google Sheets stored as a one-way hash only
License key & planTo validate your subscription statusGoogle Sheets
Transaction IDTo confirm payment and prevent fraudGoogle Sheets
Activation timestampTo calculate license expiryGoogle Sheets

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Audio recordings captured locally, processed in real-time, immediately discarded.
  • AI-generated responses or transcriptions all processing is ephemeral.
  • Keystrokes, screen content, or browsing history.
  • Credit card or banking credentials handled entirely by PhonePe.
  • Cookies for tracking, advertising, or profiling purposes.
  • IP addresses or geolocation data.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate and deliver your license key via email.
  • To validate license authenticity each time the app launches.
  • To enforce single-device binding and prevent unauthorised sharing.
  • To respond to your support and migration requests.
  • To investigate abuse or fraudulent activity.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / Indian IT Act)

  • Contract performance: Processing your email and license data is necessary to fulfil the service you purchased.
  • Legitimate interest: Storing the hardware fingerprint hash is necessary to prevent licence abuse, which would impact the sustainability of the service for all users.

6. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API Audio is sent to Whisper for transcription and to Chat Completions for answers. Processed in transit, not stored by us. Refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not share your personal information (email, hardware fingerprint) with OpenAI.
  • PhonePe Payment processing. We do not receive or store card details. Subject to PhonePe's own privacy policy.
  • Google Workspace License records are stored in Google Sheets accessible only to CloakAI. Subject to Google's privacy policy. We do not use Google Analytics or any Google advertising services.

7. Data Retention

License records (email, plan, activation data) are retained for 24 months after license expiry for audit and support purposes. They are then permanently deleted. If you request deletion before this period, your license will be deactivated as a consequence.

8. Data Security

  • Google Sheets are access-restricted to authorised CloakAI accounts only.
  • Hardware IDs are stored as SHA-256 hashes they cannot be reverse-engineered.
  • The local license cache on your device is encrypted using a machine-derived key.
  • All communication between the app and our server uses HTTPS.

9. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights by emailing support.cloakai@gmail.com:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data. Note: this will deactivate your license.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured format.

We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

CloakAI is not intended for use by persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Software after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy concerns, data requests, or questions: support.cloakai@gmail.com
