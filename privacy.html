Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026  ·  Governs all data associated with CloakAI services.

CloakAI is built on a privacy-first principle. We collect the absolute minimum data necessary to operate the service. Your audio, conversations, and AI responses are never stored by us.

1. Who We Are

CloakAI is an assistive productivity application distributed via cloakai.in. For privacy enquiries, email: support@cloakai.in. We respond 24/7 over email.

2. Data We Collect

Data TypeWhy We Collect ItWhere It's Stored
Email addressTo deliver your license key and provide supportDatabase Server (access-controlled)
Hardware fingerprint (hash)To bind your license to your device (prevents sharing)Database Server stored as a one-way hash only
License key & planTo validate your subscription statusDatabase Server

Activation timestampTo calculate license expiryDatabase Server

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We never store your audio. It is captured locally, streamed for transcription, and discarded. No copy is kept on our servers.
  • We never store transcriptions, questions or AI answers. They are passed through in real time and never written to our database. Your session transcript is saved only on your own computer, if you choose to save it.
  • Keystrokes, screen content, or browsing history.
  • Credit card or banking credentials handled entirely by PhonePe.
  • Cookies for tracking, advertising, or profiling purposes.
Important: what OpenAI does retain. Although we store none of the above, your audio and text are processed by OpenAI to produce transcriptions and answers. Under OpenAI's API terms, this data may be retained by OpenAI for up to 30 days for abuse and misuse monitoring, and is then deleted. OpenAI does not use data submitted through its API to train its models. We have no access to that copy. Please read OpenAI's API data usage policy if this matters to you.
Audio of other people, and your responsibility. When you run CloakAI in interviewer-listening mode, the audio captured and sent for transcription includes the other people on your call, who are not CloakAI users and have not agreed to our terms. You are solely responsible for ensuring your use of CloakAI is lawful where you are, including any obligation to notify or obtain consent from other participants before a call is captured or transcribed. Recording and consent laws vary by country and by state. If you are unsure, do not use the interviewer-listening mode.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate and deliver your license key via email.
  • To validate license authenticity each time the app launches.
  • To enforce single-device binding and prevent unauthorised sharing.
  • To respond to your support and migration requests.
  • To investigate abuse or fraudulent activity.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / Indian IT Act)

  • Contract performance: Processing your email and license data is necessary to fulfil the service you purchased.
  • Legitimate interest: Storing the hardware fingerprint hash is necessary to prevent licence abuse, which would impact the sustainability of the service for all users.

6. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API Audio is sent to Whisper for transcription and to Chat Completions for answers. We store none of it, but OpenAI may retain it for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring before deleting it, and does not use API data to train its models. We do not send your email address or any payment information to OpenAI. See OpenAI's API data usage policy.
  • Cloudflare All AI requests are routed through our own proxy at api.cloakai.in, running on Cloudflare Workers. This exists so the application never has to carry an AI provider credential. The proxy passes your audio and text straight through without storing them, and receives your licence key and device identifier in order to verify your subscription and meter usage. Subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) The application reports anonymous usage events so we can understand which features are used and detect errors. These carry your anonymous device identifier, plan name, app version, feature names, a count of questions asked, and error types. They never include your questions, the AI's answers, any transcription, your resume, or your email address.
  • PhonePe Payment processing. We do not receive or store card details. Subject to PhonePe's own privacy policy.
  • Google Firestore Licence records are stored in a database accessible only to CloakAI. Subject to Google's privacy policy. We do not use Google advertising services and we do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

7. Data Retention

License records (email, plan, activation data) are retained for 24 months after license expiry for audit and support purposes. They are then permanently deleted. If you request deletion before this period, your license will be deactivated as a consequence.

8. Data Security

  • Database Server are access-restricted to authorised CloakAI accounts only.
  • Hardware IDs are stored as SHA-256 hashes they cannot be reverse-engineered.
  • The local license cache on your device is encrypted using a machine-derived key.
  • All communication between the app and our server uses HTTPS.

9. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights by emailing support@cloakai.in:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data. Note: this will deactivate your license.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured format.

We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

CloakAI is not intended for use by persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Software after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy concerns, data requests, or questions: support@cloakai.in
