🎁 How the Referral System Works

1
Purchase any CloakAI plan. Your referral code is included in your license delivery email — or look it up here anytime using your license key or email.
2
Share your code with a friend who is job hunting. They enter it in the optional "Referral Code" field when activating their new license in the app.
3
Once they activate using your code, you automatically receive +1 free hour of AI time added to your account. No action needed on your end.
4
There is no limit on how many friends you can refer. Each valid referral adds another hour to your balance.

Note: Each referral code can only be used once per device. Users cannot use their own code. The +1 hour is added instantly to the referrer's account when the new user activates.
