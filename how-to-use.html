06
Listening modes

Auto AI vs Manual — pick what suits you

AUTO AI MODE
CloakAI listens continuously. When someone speaks, it records. When they stop, it answers. No button presses needed.
Best for: quiet rooms, headset mic
MANUAL MODE
Hold Alt while the question is asked, release to send. You control exactly what gets sent.
Best for: noisy rooms, precise control

Switch between modes any time by clicking the AI Answer pill in the bar. No restart needed.

07
Screen analysis

Solve on-screen coding problems with Analyze Screen

Click Analyze Screen (blue button) to capture everything on your monitor and send it to the AI. Works for LeetCode problems, shared Google Docs, system design diagrams — anything you can see but can't copy.

Tip: open the text box first (⌨), type what you want — like "Write a Python solution for this" — then click Analyze Screen. If you leave the text box empty, CloakAI describes everything it sees.

10
Interview day

Tips for your first interview

🧪
Do a dry run first
Call a friend on Zoom the day before. Test that CloakAI hears questions correctly with your chosen device.
📍
Position near your camera
Drag the overlay close to your webcam. When you glance at answers, you'll appear to look at the interviewer.
⚡
Start talking early
CloakAI streams answers word by word. Begin your response as soon as enough text appears. Don't wait for it to finish.
🫥
It's truly invisible
The interviewer's recording will not show CloakAI. Even Windows Snipping Tool cannot capture it. Hold F8 to hide if needed.
