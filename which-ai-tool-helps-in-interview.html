Which AI Tool Helps in Interview? Every Tool Reviewed for 2026

The question of which AI tool helps in interview is asked by thousands of job seekers every day — and the answer has changed dramatically in 2024 and 2026. A new generation of AI tools has moved beyond basic chatbots and practice platforms to offer genuine real-time support during live interviews. This guide covers every major category and tells you exactly which tools help, when, and how.

⚡ Quick Summary

Multiple AI tools help at different stages. For the live interview moment — the only moment that truly counts — CloakAI is the AI tool that helps most. It listens, generates answers, and stays invisible to your interviewer in real time.

Categories of AI Tools That Help in Interview

Not all AI interview tools do the same thing. Understanding the categories helps you pick the right tool for each part of your interview journey.

Category 1: Research and Preparation Tools

Tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity help you prepare before the interview. They are good at generating questions, researching companies, and helping you craft strong answers. These tools do not help during the interview itself — they are the equivalent of studying before an exam. Valuable, but not present in the room when it counts.

Category 2: Practice and Coaching Tools

Google Interview Warmup, Yoodli, and similar platforms let you practice answering questions out loud and give you feedback. They help you build the habit of articulating your experience clearly. Like Category 1, these tools are about preparation — they are not active during the real interview.

Category 3: Live Interview AI Tools

This is the most important and least populated category. Very few tools actually help during a live interview, and of those, even fewer do it invisibly. CloakAI is the leading tool in this category — designed specifically to work in real time, without the interviewer knowing it is running.

Top AI Tools That Help in Interview — Full Review

CloakAI — Best for Live Interviews

CloakAI helps in interviews by running as an invisible overlay on your screen during the actual interview. Here is exactly how it works step by step:

  1. You paste your resume into the app before the interview. This gives the AI context about your background, skills, and experience.
  2. CloakAI runs in the background while you take your video call normally on Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet.
  3. The AI listens to the conversation via microphone and audio capture. When a question is asked, it automatically begins generating an answer.
  4. You see the answer in a floating overlay that only you can see. The interviewer sees nothing — not even a blank rectangle.
  5. You speak the answer in your own words, naturally, as if you thought of it yourself.

For technical questions — coding problems shown on screen, architecture diagrams, whiteboard-style questions — the Screen Scan feature lets you point at anything on your screen and get an immediate, detailed solution.

ChatGPT — Best for Pre-Interview Prep

ChatGPT helps in interview preparation in multiple ways. Most effectively: give it the job description, your resume, and ask it to generate the 25 most likely questions for the role. Then work through each one, refining your answers over multiple sessions. ChatGPT's ability to play devil's advocate — pushing back on your answers and identifying weak points — is particularly valuable. It helps in interview preparation; it does not help during the interview itself.

Yoodli — Best for Speech Coaching

Yoodli helps in interview preparation by giving you feedback on your communication style. After a practice session, it shows you: how many filler words you used, your words-per-minute pace, how long your answers were, and how much eye contact you maintained. This is valuable data that most candidates never get. It does not help with what to say — only with how you say it.

Otter.ai / Fireflies — Best for Post-Interview Review

Transcription tools like Otter.ai or Fireflies can record and transcribe your interviews (where legally permissible). Reviewing the transcript afterward shows you exactly how you performed — which questions you answered well, where you rambled, and what the interviewer focused on. These tools help you improve for the next interview, not the current one.

LinkedIn AI Features — Best for Research

LinkedIn's AI-powered features help you research the company, understand the hiring manager's background, and see the career paths of people in similar roles. This research helps you tailor your answers and ask better questions at the end of the interview. Again, this is preparation work — not live interview support.

CloakAI vs the Current Market Leaders: Parakeet AI, Chiku AI & LockedIn AI

Parakeet AI

Parakeet AI is one of the most widely marketed AI interview tools globally, claiming 1.5 million users (self-reported). It works in real time on Zoom, Teams, and Meet with support for 52+ languages. The product is credit-based — 0.5 credits per 30-minute session — with no monthly subscription. Credits never expire.

Where it falls short for Indian candidates: The entry price is $29.50 (approximately ₹2,650) for 3 interview credits. That is over 5× the cost of CloakAI's entry plan for a similar amount of usage. Parakeet also has no free plan — you cannot test it before paying. Its privacy claims ("undetectable") are prominent in marketing but rely on the same mechanisms most AI tools claim, without the OS-level content protection CloakAI uses on Windows. Answers are not as deeply personalised to your specific resume as CloakAI's.

Parakeet AI vs CloakAI verdict: Parakeet is a credible tool with strong multilingual support, but for Indian job seekers it is significantly overpriced. CloakAI offers the same core real-time functionality at a fraction of the cost, with a genuinely free 20-minute trial and deeper resume personalisation.

Chiku AI

Chiku AI is the most direct Indian market competitor to CloakAI. It positions itself as "69% cheaper than Parakeet AI" and targets Indian candidates with a "Desi Mode" — answers tuned to how Indian engineers actually speak in interviews. It supports 52+ languages, handles coding rounds, and uses a credit-based model starting at ₹1,199 (+GST).

Where it falls short: The entry price of ₹1,199 + GST (approximately ₹1,415 all-in) still works out far costlier per hour than CloakAI's ₹999 for 3 hours (₹333 per AI hour). More critically, Chiku AI is a browser-based tool — it runs in Chrome rather than as a native desktop application. Browser-based tools are significantly more detectable during screen sharing because browser windows appear in screen capture by default and tab-switching is visible. CloakAI uses native Windows content protection at the OS level, which browser-based tools fundamentally cannot replicate. Chiku also has a smaller user base (~10K candidates) and limited third-party review presence.

Chiku AI vs CloakAI verdict: Chiku AI gets the India-first positioning right, but the browser-based architecture is a real limitation on screenshare protection. CloakAI is cheaper, genuinely invisible at the OS level, and has a proper 20-minute free trial. For Indian candidates, CloakAI is the better value by both price and reliability.

LockedIn AI

LockedIn AI is a broad career platform with 14+ tools — resume builder, job auto-apply, headshot generator, and an interview copilot. It has a free tier and premium plans starting at $49.99/month (approximately ₹4,200/month). The platform claims an ultra-fast 116ms response time and offers a unique "LockedIn Duo" feature that lets a trusted contact assist you during your interview in real time.

Where it falls short: At $49.99/month, it is one of the most expensive options on the market for regular use. More importantly, user reviews note that LockedIn AI is browser-based and has reported visibility issues — multiple independent reviewers have flagged that interviewers can see the interface when tabs are switched or the screen is shared. The breadth of features (14+ tools) is impressive on paper but means the live interview copilot itself is not the primary focus. CloakAI does one thing and does it properly: invisible real-time AI during live interviews.

LockedIn AI vs CloakAI verdict: If you want a broad career platform with many tools, LockedIn AI covers a lot of ground. If you want the most reliable invisible live interview AI at the lowest cost, CloakAI wins clearly — no subscription, no visibility risk, no monthly commitment, and a free trial.
